Evidence Stage Definitions
RCT
Randomized Controlled Trial
Gold-standard causal evaluation with treatment and control groups. Strongest evidence of program impact.
Mature
Mature Evidence Base
Multiple evaluations over time with strong outcome data. Not necessarily RCT-validated.
Growth
Evidence in Development
Promising outcomes but evidence still developing as the program scales.
Descriptive
Self-Reported / Descriptive
Outcomes reported by the program without independent evaluation. Useful signal; treat with caution.
Pending
Research Pending
Program is in the registry but evidence data has not yet been researched or populated.
